Experience the thrill of classic first-person shooter action reimagined for modern platforms. VIOLENCE is a raycasting FPS that pays homage to the golden age of corridor shooters while introducing innovative procedural generation and cross-platform multiplayer.

KEY FEATURES:

• Five Unique Genres: Choose from Fantasy, Sci-Fi, Horror, Cyberpunk, or Post-Apocalyptic themes—each with distinct visual atmosphere and thematic elements

• Procedurally Generated Levels: Every playthrough offers new challenges with seed-based deterministic generation ensuring reproducible experiences

• Classic Raycasting Engine: Authentic Wolfenstein-style rendering with modern enhancements including floor/ceiling casting, sprite billboarding, and atmospheric distance fog

• Multiplayer Modes: Engage in cooperative missions or competitive deathmatch with cross-platform federation

• Complete Customization: Remap controls, adjust FOV and sensitivity, select your preferred genre theme

• Cloud Save Sync: Continue your progress across devices with encrypted cloud save support for S3 and WebDAV backends

• Mod Support: Extend gameplay with WASM-based mods featuring secure sandboxing

• Achievement System: Track your progress across multiple difficulty levels and game modes

• Leaderboards: Compete globally with anti-cheat protected rankings

• Replay System: Record and share your best runs with deterministic playback

PROCEDURALLY GENERATED UNIVERSE:

All gameplay assets—including audio, visuals, and narrative elements—are generated at runtime using deterministic algorithms. No pre-rendered content means infinite variety within each thematic genre.

GAMEPLAY MODES:

Single-Player Campaign: Navigate procedurally generated levels, collect keycards, discover secrets, and master each genre's unique atmosphere.

Co-op: Team up with friends for shared adventures in any genre setting.

Deathmatch: Compete against other players in fast-paced arena combat.

TECHNICAL EXCELLENCE:

Built with performance in mind, VIOLENCE maintains smooth framerates even on older devices while delivering rich atmospheric experiences through efficient raycasting technology.
