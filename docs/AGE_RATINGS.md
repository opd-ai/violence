## Content Justifications

### Why TEEN/12 and not MATURE/18?
1. **Stylized Graphics**: Raycasting graphics are non-photorealistic (retro aesthetic)
2. **No Gore**: No dismemberment, decapitation, or graphic injury depictions
3. **Minimal Blood**: Particle effects only, no blood pools or persistent stains
4. **Fantasy Context**: Sci-fi setting with alien/robot enemies (not realistic human violence)
5. **No Extreme Language**: No scripted profanity (user chat is filtered)
6. **No Sexual Content**: Completely absent
7. **No Substance Abuse**: No drugs, alcohol, or tobacco depiction

### Why not EVERYONE/7?
1. **Combat is Core Gameplay**: Violence is central mechanic (not educational/puzzle)
2. **Frequent Weapon Use**: Players use guns/weapons throughout game
3. **Blood Effects Present**: Even minimal blood requires TEEN/12 minimum
4. **Multiplayer Interaction**: Unmoderated user communication (despite filters)
